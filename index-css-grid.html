Our Story

We are El Masry girls. Our names are Hadeer and Areej El Masry. We are originally from Alexandria, Egypt. We moved to the USA in 2016.

We love photography as it makes us appreciate every single moment & the beauty of everything around us. It allows us to connect with people by capturing their spontaneous actions.

The best feeling ever when we draw a smile on our client’s face & getting great feedback from them.

Our clients always comment that our pictures are artistic, professional, spontaneous and full of details which we believe it gives a big value to the outcome of every photo shoot we take!
